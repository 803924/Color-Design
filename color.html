Color Scheme Examples


There are a lot of different and unique color schemes that give a variety of different feelings. Choosing the right one can make all the difference in how your user interacts with your website.

Hover over images to increase size

Colorful and balanced

Image from Adult Swim Singles andVisme.

Warm and cool hues are combined in this gently colorful palette. With a bright bluish-green to an earthy terracotta, this color scheme is well suited for a youthful and modern design


Stylish and Sophisticated

Image from Fay and Visme.

This elegant scheme puts together dark muted tones to create a clean and sophisticated look best for conservative designs.


Deep Purples and Blues

Image from Arkade and Visme.

This mysterious dark color scheme with vibrant blue accent follows The trend of the use of dark background colors with bright and bold accent colors.


Innovative and Audacious

Image from Assiko and Visme.

This combination of portland orange, yellow and jade on dark gray screams for your attention. Full of energy, This color scheme is perfect for a contemporary and audacious look.


Loud and In-Your-Face

Image from Panic and Visme.

unlike the other schemes that were eye-catching but not too bright, this one is purposely loud to the point where it could scare off some users. If used correctly though, it can work well when trying to make a bold statement.
